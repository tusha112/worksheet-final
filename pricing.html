Frequently asked questions

Can I switch between plans? ›
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.
Is there a free trial on Pro? ›
Yes! Every Pro plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you accept? ›
We accept all major credit/debit cards, ACH transfers, and invoicing for Enterprise customers.
Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits? ›
Absolutely. We offer 50% off for qualifying startups and non-profits. Contact us to apply.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ›
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, giving you time to export everything. After that, it is securely deleted.
